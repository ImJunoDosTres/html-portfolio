The Best Movies According to Angelo

My top 3 movies of all time.

Train to Busan

"Train to Busan" is a South Korean zombie thriller that blends intense action with emotional depth. brThe film follows a father and daughter as they face a zombie outbreak during a train journey to Busan. The movie skillfully balances heart-pounding suspense with well-developed characters and explores themes of survival, human connection, and social commentary. The film's expert pacing, stellar performances, and thought-provoking storytelling make it a standout in the zombie genre, appealing to both fans of the genre and those seeking a compelling cinematic experience.

Gods of Egypt

"Gods of Egypt" is a visually striking fantasy film set in an ancient Egypt filled with gods and mortals. The story follows mortal hero Bek as he teams up with the god Horus to overthrow the tyrannical god Set. The film's impressive visual effects and imaginative world-building stand out, but its storytelling is uneven, with convoluted plot elements and pacing issues. Controversies over casting choices also impact the film's cultural authenticity. Despite these drawbacks, the movie offers captivating action scenes and a unique blend of mythology and fantasy that might appeal to fans of the genre.
